About Us

Alpha-Springs is fast growing firm established in Nigeria as a grassroot Educational Consulting organization focused on assisting students from all over the world to secure admission to schools of choice, obtain requisite travel visas and fully explore other available goals.

Established in 2018, Alpha-Springs has progressively grown into one of the most trusted agencies in the world of educational services consultancy.

Our Canada office offers only educational consultancy

Services We Offer

Educational Consultancy

Our main goal is to help students, as well as professionals, who have completed all or part of their education outside the US to reach their educational and professional goals. We do this through the process called Credential Evaluation.

International Exam Facilitators

Our College admission service involves students' placement at both undergraduate and graduate level to any program in any regionally accredited higher education institution in the US. We work with applicants interested in study abroad programs one semester / one year (Gap Year) in the UK and Canada.
